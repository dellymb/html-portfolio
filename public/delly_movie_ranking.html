My Top 3 Movies of All Times

War of the Worlds

War of the Worlds is a science fiction movie about a father who tries to protect his children during an alien invasion. Strange machines rise from the ground and begin destroying cities, forcing people to run for safety. The movie focuses on survival, fear, and the importance of family during dangerous situations.

Inception

Inception tells the story of a skilled thief who enters people’s dreams to steal secrets. He is given a difficult mission to plant an idea into someone’s mind instead. The movie is full of action, creative dream worlds, and complex ideas about reality and imagination.

The Terminal

The Terminal follows a man who becomes trapped in an airport after his country faces political problems. Unable to leave, he learns to live inside the terminal and builds friendships with the staff. The movie shows kindness, patience, and hope in difficult situations.
